Wilbert Pol's extended Mooneye test suite (mooneye-test-suite-wilbertpol).
Based on Mooneye GB by Joonas Javanainen; MIT. Uses the illegal opcode 0xED
as its completion breakpoint (vs Mooneye's LD B,B). Only DMG tests that add
NEW coverage over the standard Mooneye suite are vendored here (the SCX
mode-0-timing variants, which exercise the mode-3 SCX fine-scroll penalty).
